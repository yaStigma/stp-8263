FAQ

Is the game free to play?

Yes, most levels can be played offline, but certain events and social features require an internet connection

Can I play offline?

Yes! Seaside Resort: Match 3 is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases.

How do I unlock new resort areas?

Complete levels, earn stars, and use them to renovate and expand your resort!

Are there real-world resort inspirations?

Absolutely! The game's locations are inspired by real tropical resorts and stunning coastal destinations.
