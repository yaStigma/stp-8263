About game

Escape to paradise in Seaside Resort: Match 3! Solve engaging match-3 puzzles to design and expand your dream beachfront resort. Unlock breathtaking locations, decorate tropical villas, and discover hidden treasures along the shore. Whether you're a puzzle master or a casual gamer, this relaxing adventure will transport you to a world of sun, sand, and endless fun!

Key Features

  • Relaxing Match 3 Gameplay: Match seashells, starfish, and tropical fruits to complete puzzles!

  • Resort Building & Customization: Unlock and decorate luxurious beachfront locations.

  • Exciting Challenges & Rewards: Solve unique puzzles and earn rewards to upgrade your resort.

  • Tropical Atmosphere: Enjoy stunning visuals, ocean waves, and soothing beach sounds.

  • Engaging Storyline: Meet interesting characters and uncover secrets hidden in the resort.

  • Daily Events & Leaderboards: Compete in challenges and earn exclusive rewards!

FAQ

Is the game free to play?

Yes, most levels can be played offline, but certain events and social features require an internet connection

Can I play offline?

Yes! Seaside Resort: Match 3 is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases.

How do I unlock new resort areas?

Complete levels, earn stars, and use them to renovate and expand your resort!

Are there real-world resort inspirations?

Absolutely! The game's locations are inspired by real tropical resorts and stunning coastal destinations.
